ABOUT UNIVERSITY
Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a private university in Phagwara, Punjab, India. It was established in 2005 by the Lovely International Trust through the Lovely Professional University Act, 2005 (Punjab Act No. 25 of 2005). It is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) above all other universities in India.
LPU is one of the largest single-campus universities in india, with over 30,000 students from all over india and 70 other countries. The univrsity offers more than 200 programs at various levels, including diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral degrees in various field such as engineering, management, law,arts, science, and more.


ABOUT EDUCATION IN LPU
LPU is known for its modern and innovative approach to education and its focus on research and development. The university has state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, libraries, and smart classrooms. LPU emphasizes experiential learning, industry collaborations, and skill development to prepare students for global careers. The university also encourages participation in research projects, internships, and extracurricular activities to foster holistic growth.
